📦 Stocky – Easy Inventory

Simple, local, and private inventory management.

Stocky is an app designed for those who need to manage their inventory quickly and easily. From tools and materials to homemade products, Stocky offers an efficient solution right from your phone.

🚀 Main Features

  • ✅ Easily add, edit, and delete items
  • 📁 Organize your inventory by categories
  • 🔍 Fast search by name or code
  • 🧾 View and export your data
  • ☁️ Cloud synchronization with Firebase - Your data is always safe
  • 👤 User accounts - Personalized experience across devices
  • 📧 Optional promotional emails - Stay updated with new features
  • 🔄 Sync with Google Sheets (Premium version)
  • 📱 Free version with ads

💎 Premium Version

Make a one-time payment of €5 to unlock:

  • 🚫 Remove all ads
  • 📊 Advanced analytics
  • 📤 Export to different formats
  • 📦 Have more than 25 products
  • 📸 Upload photos to your products

👤 Who is it for?

  • Freelancers or small businesses
  • Makers and creators managing materials or stock
  • Individuals who want to inventory personal items
  • Users who value cloud backup and cross-device synchronization

🛠️ Technologies Used

  • Flutter – Cross-platform app
  • Firebase – Cloud database and authentication
  • Google Sheets API – Optional sync (Premium)
  • Google AdMob – Ad system (free version)

🔐 Privacy & Data

Stocky uses Firebase to provide you with the best experience:

  • 🔒 Your data is securely stored in Google's Firebase cloud
  • 👤 User accounts allow personalization and data recovery
  • 📧 Optional promotional emails (you can opt-out anytime)
  • 🔄 Automatic backup ensures you never lose your inventory
  • 📱 Access your data from any device
