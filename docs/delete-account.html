🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! If you need to delete your Stocky account, please follow these simple steps:

📱 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Open the Stocky app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (usually found in the main menu or profile section)
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings page
  4. Tap "Delete Account" option
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

⚠️ Important Information

Before deleting your account, please note:

  • 🗑️ All your data will be permanently deleted - This includes all your inventory items, categories, and photos
  • ⏰ This action cannot be undone - Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered
  • 📧 You will stop receiving emails from us (including promotional emails if you were subscribed)
  • 💾 Consider exporting your data first if you want to keep a backup

📤 Export Your Data First (Recommended)

If you want to keep a copy of your inventory data before deleting your account:

  • Go to Settings → Export Data
  • Choose your preferred format (CSV, PDF, etc.)
  • Save the exported file to your device
  • Then proceed with account deletion

🔄 Alternative: Temporary Break

If you're not sure about permanently deleting your account, consider:

  • 📧 Unsubscribing from emails instead of deleting your account
  • 🔕 Disabling notifications in the app settings
  • 📱 Simply not using the app for a while - your data will remain safe

❓ Need Help?

If you're having trouble finding the delete option or have questions about account deletion, please contact us:

📧 contact@stockyinventory.com

Include "Account Deletion Help" in your email subject line for faster assistance.

🔒 Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously. When you delete your account:

  • ✅ All personal data is removed from our servers within 30 days
  • ✅ Your email is immediately removed from all mailing lists
  • ✅ Any cached data is cleared from our systems
  • ✅ Third-party integrations (if any) are disconnected
